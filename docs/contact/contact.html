Contact

The archive handles inquiries across five channels. Please use the one that best matches what you're reaching out about — it helps messages reach the right person quickly.

Project Director

Harry M. Foster directs the Detroit Badman Digital Archive. Reach out here for questions about the archive's scholarly direction, research collaborations, or dissertation-related inquiries.

Harry M. Foster
Doctoral Candidate, Department of English
Michigan State University
director@badmandigitalarchive.com

General Inquiries

For questions about the archive's content, corrections to existing entries, or anything that doesn't fit one of the channels below, general inquiries are the right path.

info@badmandigitalarchive.com

Press and Media

Journalists, documentarians, and media producers working on stories that reference the archive or its figures should reach out here. The archive is happy to provide background, fact-checking support, or interview availability on a case-by-case basis.

press@badmandigitalarchive.com

Community Partnerships

Detroit organizations, cultural institutions, and community groups interested in hosting an event, collaborating on digitization, or building a formal partnership with the archive can reach out through this channel.

partnerships@badmandigitalarchive.com

Submissions

Most submissions should go through the Submit a Figure form, which collects the information the review pipeline needs in the order it needs it. This channel exists for questions about the submission process itself, status inquiries on pending submissions, or requests for accommodations if the standard form doesn't work for you.

submissions@badmandigitalarchive.com

Response times vary with volume. Most messages receive a reply within two weeks.
